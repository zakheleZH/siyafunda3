More Than A Decade Of Educational Impact

Syafunda, meaning "We Are Learning", was founded in 2013 with a vision to expand access to quality education through innovative digital solutions.

Founded by Zakheni Ngubo, Syafunda emerged from a simple but powerful belief: every learner deserves access to quality education regardless of geography, income, or circumstance.
